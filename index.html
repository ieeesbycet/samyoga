About SAMYOGA

The Arts Festival at Younus College of Engineering and Technology, known as Samyoga, is a much-anticipated event that serves as a unifying force, bringing together all departments and students within the institution. Samyoga stands as a testament to the rich cultural diversity and creativity present at YCET, and it plays a pivotal role in fostering a sense of community, togetherness, and unity among YCETians.The festival transcends the boundaries of academic disciplines, encouraging students from various departments to participate, collaborate, and showcase their artistic talents. It serves as a platform where students can celebrate their successes.
